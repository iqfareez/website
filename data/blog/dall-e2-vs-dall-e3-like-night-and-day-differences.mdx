On September 2023, OpenAI announced new text-to-image model, [Dall·E 3](https://openai.com/dall-e-3). I first got the oppurtunity to try
Dall e 3 when my company give access to ChatGPT Pro, which include the Dall E 3 model. I am pretty excited because the
image generated is far better than Dall·E 2 that I've explored with some times ago.

So today, I create a simple [python program](https://github.com/iqfareez/openai-dall-e3) and incorporated the OpenAI's Image Generation API to redo the prompt from
previous Dall·E 2 experiment. Note that the Dall·E 2 images shows below are generated on November 2022.

Below is the image comparison of the Dall·E 2 and Dall·E 3 side-by side. _Sorry for the inconsistent image sizes._
